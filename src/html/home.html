Recommended Books

Celebrate Christmas in July

Or even without celebrating, enjoy this classic Charles Dickens tale and the timeless characters of Scrooge, Marley, and the ghosts of Christmas Past, Present, and Future. And examine your own life along the way.

The Magic of a Garden

Enjoy the reading magic of seeing grouchy young Mary (quite contrary) change as she works in a garden and makes friends along the way. These characters will seem like your friends over time spent with them and give you a youthful view on things.

Not Just a Musical

While this book is known for its wonderful musical (which I recommend), this book is not to be overlooked. Great characters and development, stay with your favorite characters even longer in the form of a 1000+ page book (although, we do note, there are long battle scenes in the book which can be dry) but it's totally worth reading!
